Triploid atlantic salmon

Quentin Andres
May 2021
PDF Cite Slides

Abstract

The farmed atlantic salmon can be made sterile while in the triploid state. Triploidy and induced sterility is a great tool in order to create environment-friendly salmon aqua-farming.
